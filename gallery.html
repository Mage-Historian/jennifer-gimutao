ACOUSTIC GUITAR GALLERY

Below are the different kinds of Acoustic Guitar:

Name: Dreadnought Guitar
Creator: Martin (1916)
Dimensions: 20 (L) X 16 (B); Scale Length: 25.4
Also Known as: Common Guitar

Name: Jumbo Guitar
Creator: Gibson (late 1930s)
Dimensions: (Expected width) up to 17" in the lower bout
Also Known as: J-200

Name: Auditorium Guitar
Creator: Martins (early 1920s)
Trait: more defined midrange
Also Known as: Orchestra, OOO-28

Name: Parlor Guitar
Creators: Fender, Washburn, Ibanez (late 1800s): Parlor Body Shape: C.F. Martins (19th Century
Features: Less Bass, More Midranged Emphasis

Name: Classical Guitar
Features: Nylon string tone, soft tone

Name: Electric Guitar
Inventors: Adolph Rickenbacker and George Beauchamp
Features: pickup, amplifier.
